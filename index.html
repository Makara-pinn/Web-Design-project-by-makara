This school in 2015 in Phnom Penh, School Coffee Adventure (SCA) offers a 2-year training program, based on a holistic approach including coffee business skills and professional (soft) skills.

In 2017, the school opened its doors and accepted 20 students for the first semester, with 50 students selected. In 2020, the school provided scholarships to poor students and expanded recruitment and outreach.

Our training program

Explore more about our training

Coffee Adventure has implemented an innovative and comprehensive training program in each of its centers, focused on long-term employability.

Coffee Adventure in Cambodia (SCA) offers a 2-year full-time training program with a major in meeting the needs of local companies in the industry, for underprivileged Cambodian youths.

At the end of the training, students receive an Associate Degree in Computer Science with a major in coffee business, as well as a Coffee Adventure school certificate.
