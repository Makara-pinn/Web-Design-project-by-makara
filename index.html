Our Training Program

Coffee Adventure has implemented an innovative and comprehensive training program in each of its centers, focused on long-term employability.

An associate degree in coffee sector

Coffe Adventure in Cambodia (SCA) offers a 2-year full time
training team with a major in meeting the needs of local companies
in the industry, for underprivileged Cambodian youths.

At the end of the training, students receive an Associate Degree
in Computer Science with a major in coffee business, as well as a
coffee Adventure school certificate.

Throughout their training, students benefit from a comprehensive
professional and technical skills training, and strong support for their
professional integration. They also participate in extra-curricular and
personal development activities.
